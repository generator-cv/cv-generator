Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

ProCV Generator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free online CV generator at generator-cv.github.io. We are based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, and serve users across East Africa and beyond.

2. Data We Collect

For users who use the CV generator without an account, all data you enter (name, email, work history, etc.) exists only in your browser's memory. We do not transmit or store this data on any server. It disappears when you close or refresh your browser tab.

For users who create a free account, we store the following information locally in your browser's localStorage: your name, email address, phone number, and any CVs you choose to save. This data never leaves your device — it is not stored on our servers.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit our site and which pages are most popular. This analytics data is anonymous and does not identify individual users. We do not use advertising cookies or sell any user data to third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the CV generation service
  • To save your CVs locally for future editing (account users only)
  • To respond to your contact form inquiries
  • To improve our service based on aggregate usage patterns

5. Data Security

Since all CV data is stored locally in your browser (not on our servers), the security of your data depends on the security of your personal device. We recommend using a private or personal device when building your CV and downloading your PDF promptly after completion.

6. Premium CV Service

When you order a Premium CV design through WhatsApp, you will share your CV content with Hafidh Omar Othman for the purpose of designing your CV. This information is used solely to create your CV and is not shared with any third parties. We retain your information only as long as necessary to complete the service.

Career Tips

Expert CV Writing Tips

Proven advice to help your CV stand out and land interviews in 2026.

💡
Loading your first tip…
Tip 1 of 10
🎯

Tailor Every Application

Customize your CV for each role you apply to. Match keywords from the job description to pass ATS filters.

📊

Quantify Your Achievements

"Increased team efficiency by 40%" beats "improved team performance" every single time.

✍️

Keep It Concise — 1 or 2 Pages

Recruiters spend 6–7 seconds on initial review. Make every word earn its place on the page.

🔍

Proofread Thoroughly

A single spelling mistake can disqualify you. Read backwards, then ask someone else to review it.

📧

Use a Professional Email

firstname.lastname@gmail.com is ideal. Old or quirky email handles signal carelessness.

💼

Lead With Strong Action Verbs

Start every bullet: "Led", "Designed", "Launched", "Achieved". These words project confidence.

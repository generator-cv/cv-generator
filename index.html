Our Story & Mission

Built in Dar es Salaam for job seekers across East Africa.

We started ProCV to solve a real problem

In 2024, Hafidh Omar Othman — a developer based in Dar es Salaam — noticed that thousands of talented job seekers across Tanzania and East Africa were losing opportunities simply because they didn't have access to professional CV tools.

So he built ProCV Generator — a powerful, free, browser-based CV builder with 5 professional templates and photo upload, tailored specifically for the East African job market.
